by John Wilkes, Jeffrey Mogul, and Jaap Suermondt

This paper elaborates on utilitfication - the transfer of applications to utility computing. Utility computing provides reliable, resilient, scalable provision of computer-based services, as and when they are needed, in response to internal or external events.
