by Hausheer et al.

This is a very general high level article on the Future Internet which identifies issues like

  • scalability and address space limitations
  • balance of 'privacy' versus 'sharing'
  • need for security
  • trust as a major issue
  • customization

Finally, the article notes that different initiatives towards the Future Internet (EU, US, Korea) are pursued by different organizations which might lead to a market of competing Future Internets.
